The Third Ear

A platform for building fluid, goal-directed voice interviewers

Current data collection often relies on self-report surveys, capturing limited information and leaving much of the narrative untold. Face-to-face interviews yield more accurate and comprehensive data, but are time-consuming and expensive. Our mission at Third Ear is to revolutionize this process by leveraging advanced audio analysis and natural language processing technologies. We aim to provide a scalable, cost-effective solution that maintains the richness and depth of personal interviews, enabling organizations to gain deeper insights and more accurate data than ever before.
