The 3rd Ear

A platform for building fluid, goal-directed voice interviewers

  • Face-to-face interviews yield more accurate and comprehensive data than surveys, but are time-consuming to conduct and expensive to analyze.
  • By focusing on interviewing rather than offering answers or advice, we minimize the risks of AI hallucinations and bias.
  • The platform orchestrates adaptive, goal-directed dialogue to produce rich interview data, enabling organizations to gain deeper insights and more accurate data than ever before.
