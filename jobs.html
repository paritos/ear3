Job Title: Junior Software Engineer (Front-End Focus with Python Skills)

Location: Remote or Hybrid (HQ in San Francisco)

Type: Contractor

About Us

We are an ambitious voice AI startup revolutionizing how humans and technology interact through natural language. We’re building cutting-edge voice-driven experiences that power the future of AI communication. Our team values creativity, passion, and a drive for solving complex challenges. We are committed to fostering an inclusive environment where diverse perspectives thrive.

Role Overview

We’re looking for a Junior Software Engineer to join our growing team. You will work closely with our back-end, and voice AI specialists to build intuitive user experiences and seamless integrations with our backend APIs. This is a great opportunity for someone with diverse front-end experience and solid Python skills to grow in a collaborative and fast-paced startup environment.

What You’ll Do

  • Collaborate with our engineering team to develop front-end features for our AI-powered platform.
  • Integrate and interact with backend APIs to ensure cohesive data flow and enhance the overall user experience.
  • Write, test, and maintain Python code in our core codebase, helping to enhance existing features or create new ones.
  • Work alongside senior engineers to contribute to the development and maintenance of both client-facing and internal tools.
  • Participate in code reviews and bring your unique perspective to help us improve and innovate.

What We’re Looking For

  • 1-2 years of experience in front-end development using frameworks like React, Vue, Flutter, or Angular.
  • Hands-on experience working with REST APIs, understanding how data flows between systems.
  • Proficiency in Python – comfortable with scripting, writing backend services, or extending our existing codebase.
  • A willingness to learn and a proactive attitude towards problem-solving.
  • Strong communication skills and an openness to feedback – we love collaboration!
  • Familiarity with version control tools like Git.

Nice-to-Haves

  • Exposure to cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP).
  • Experience working in an agile environment or with voice AI technologies.
  • Knowledge of design principles and UX best practices.
  • Interest in voice user interface (VUI) design and conversational AI.

How to Apply

If you’re excited about building engaging voice experiences and want to work at a company that values growth, diversity, and impactful tech, we’d love to hear from you!

To apply, please submit your resume and a short cover letter highlighting your experience and why you’re excited to join our team.

System2 is an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive environment for all employees.
